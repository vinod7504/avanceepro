Statutory compliance Review

Statutory compliance has become critical and important in the recent times from an organizational perspective as well as from the director's responsibility viewpoint. Also the cost of default/non-compliance can be heavy in terms of financial and other obligations.

In view of the above, it is necessary that statutory health checkups at periodical intervals are carried out to avoid any surprises/non-compliance.

We undertake review of books of accounts and report the state of statutory compliance at periodical intervals to the management/BOD. We undertake statutory compliance review on a quarterly basis.

This includes review/verification of:

  • TDS related compliances.
  • GST related compliances.
  • Routine Income Tax compliances.
  • Compliances in respect of Labour related laws like Provident Fund, ESI, and Professional Tax etc.
  • Shops and Establishment Act compliance.

Migration of books of accounts into ERP System

Enterprise Resource Planning (ERP) systems are software packages that use relational database technology to integrate various units of an organization's information system. ERP systems provide several separate, but integrated modules, which can be installed as a package for any organization

We have experience in offering services in the field of migration of books of accounts into ERP system. We will identify, review, and provide recommendations for key controls associated with the project and can provide assurance that the ERP system will support business processes and enforce business controls on an ongoing basis.

The major advantages of implementation of ERP system are scalability, improved reporting, data quality with low cost of maintenance, improved data access and Regulatory compliance etc.

There are many more benefits of an ERP system, but these are the chief ones. Needless to say, a good ERP system is indispensable in the modern economic scenario.
